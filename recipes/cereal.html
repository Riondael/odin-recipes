Cereal

Description

This is a recipe for a simple bowl of Cereal

Cereal can be a great start to your day, depending on the type of cereal you choose. If you're going for a healthy breakfast, there are a few types of cereal you might want to choose from. There will be a list of types you might want below, followed by how to make them. Otherwise, you might want a sugary concoction that will taste like you're on a rainbow. There are also a few options listed below. Just choose your type and follow the steps below to make a delicious bowl of cereal!

Healthy

  • Honey Nut Cheerios
  • Raisin Bran(My favorite)
  • Corn Flakes

Side Note: All of these choices are filled with Fiber and can help you feel full longer. Cheerios also boast that they're heart healthy, but take that with a grain of salt.

Sugary

  • Frosted Flakes
  • Captain Crunch
  • Reese's Puffs

Side Note: Be wary of eating too much of these as it can lead to pallette fatigue and you could also crash from all the sugar. Eat at your own discretion.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Pour a generous amount of healthy cereal, otherwise a small portion of sugary cereal, into a bowl
  2. Pour the desired amount of milk or until the cereal floats to the top of the bowl
  3. Use a spoon to scoop the cereal and shove it into your mouth!
