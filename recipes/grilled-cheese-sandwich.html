Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for the Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Everyone knows that the grilled cheese is a classic recipe, due to it's simple and delicious punch! There are a few reasons why you'll love this recipe.

First of all, it's cheap and easy to make. My favorite type is using the Kraft American Singles due to how cheap they are, but they're also delicious. This recipe is not just good because it's cheap and delicious though. It's also great because you can add whatever cheese you would like, as well as the type of bread. You could get as fancy as using cheddar, but you could also use mozzarella. Now, some cheese is better for use due to its' melting capabilities. This is why I prefer the Kraft Singles. Bread is also a factor. You can use whole-grain, white, or whatever type you'd like. Although, for this recipe (due to simplicity reasons), we will be sticking with my version of a good grilled cheese sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of White Bread
  • 2 tsp. of Salted Butter
  • 2 Slices of Kraft American Singles

Directions

  1. Heat your pan on medium-high for 3 minutes
  2. While your pan is heating, butter both slices of bread on one side
  3. Once the pan has heated, add one slice of bread onto the pan (butter side down). Then stack the slices of cheese on top, followed by the last slice of bread (butter side up)
  4. Push down on the sandwich using a spatula in order to allow the cheese to melt more quickly
  5. Allow the sandwich to brown on one side, then flip and repeat the browning process.
  6. Once both sides are brown and crisp, ensure that the cheese is melted to your own liking, and serve!
